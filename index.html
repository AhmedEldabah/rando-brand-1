Our Story

Passion Brewed
into Every Cup

Noir was born from a simple obsession: the perfect cup of coffee. Our founder travelled to Ethiopia, Colombia, and Japan to learn from the world's finest roasters before opening our doors in 2019.

Today, we source single-origin beans from over 12 farms, roast in small batches every morning, and serve each cup as a handcrafted experience — never rushed, never compromised.
